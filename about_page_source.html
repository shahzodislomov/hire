Story

How I've got here

I discovered programming at the age of 8 thanks to my father, through an old Dreamweaver 3 book. However, my first serious project came at 14, when I built a website for a local company.

I have witnessed the constant evolution of the web paradigm - from static websites with very limited design and user experience capabilities, to the groundbreaking rise of jQuery at the time, and later the emergence of powerful frameworks that fundamentally reshaped how we think about and build software. That transformation continues to this day.

I have collaborated with multiple major companies in the sector, contributing and learning extensively while building a strong foundation in a product-oriented mindset.

We are currently living through an extraordinary shift driven by artificial intelligence. While many see it as a threat to the software industry, I see it as an opportunity. I focus on exploring and embracing these emerging technologies to create smarter, more effective solutions, combining them with my real-world experience in the field.

Values

What drives me

  • Evolution of technology

    We cannot fight it; instead, we must embrace these emerging technologies as tools that help us reach new levels of innovation and tackle increasingly complex problems.

  • Product-driven development

    The functionality and objectives of the product must come first. When orchestrating AI agents, one of the most important aspects is maintaining well-segmented documentation across the entire project, along with clearly defined business logic that is accessible and structured in an agent-oriented format.

  • Auditory and security

    When discussing automation and autonomy in the orchestration of multiple agents developing our products, we must ensure a robust audit system that guarantees both product quality and security.
